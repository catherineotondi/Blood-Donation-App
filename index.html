ABOUT US

GlobalBlood Donation is an organization that is geared to support hospitals and charitable organizations by connectiong them to donors and respective companies when need be.

Individual volunteers are required to use this platform to render their donation details that is geared to narrow down blood donors search during emergencies.This helps to reduce the number of death occurrences in various countries as result saving alot of lives.

The GlobalBlood Donation has registered with wide range of hospitals globally for volunteers to conduct their test freely and have their test result verified before any contact.

Our impact goes beyond moving money to where it’s needed most; it’s also about putting communities in the driver’s seat of change and shifting power to transform the way aid and philanthropy work.
